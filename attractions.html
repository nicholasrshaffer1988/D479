What to do

Attractions

Culture, outdoors, and reef spots worth your sunscreen.

Cultural

  • City Market Walk: morning stalls and crafts
  • Museum of Taniti: history and natural exhibits
  • Old Town Tour: architecture and lookout

Outdoor

  • Rainforest Hike: shaded loop, local flora
  • Volcano Vista: shuttle to scenic overlook
  • Cliff Lookout: sunset views, picnic area

Snorkeling

  • Reef Garden: calm lagoon, beginner friendly
  • Turtle Cove: frequent turtle sightings

Dining

Street food by the market, seaside restaurants on Harbor Road, and late-night spots near the marina.
