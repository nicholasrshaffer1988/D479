Visit Taniti

Markets, museums, reefs, rainforest. One trip, zero rush.

Plan your trip Browse attractions

Culture

City Market Walk, Museum of Taniti, and Old Town architecture with guided tours daily.

Outdoors

Rainforest hikes, volcano viewpoints, reef snorkeling, and coastal bike paths.

Food & Nightlife

Seafood stands, Pan-Asian cafes, family restaurants, plus a few harbor night spots.

Know before you go

Getting there

Daily flights; ferry twice a week. Taxis and rental bikes on arrival.

Lodging

Harbor hotels, guesthouses, and eco-lodges. Book early in peak season.

Accessibility

Step-free access at major sites, marked curb cuts, priority bus seating.

Food

Seafood, rice dishes, tropical fruit. Common allergens labeled at most venues.

Safety

Low crime. Follow posted reef guidance; currents vary by cove.

Best time

Dry season for calm seas; shoulder months are cheaper and quieter.
